About

VITO BUSINESS CONSOLIDATED SYNERGY LIMITED is a company duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission to carry on the business of general contractors, consultation services, training and capacity building, strategic solutions and other relevant business activities.


We are established with sustainability mindset and with the target to reduce, if not eradicate the challenge of having abandoned infrastructural projects due to funds mismanagement and misappropriation; ensure that empowerment programmes that are funded by government officials, agencies or well-meaning individuals from the private sector are effectively carried out as supposed, and to undertake contracts with these entities so as to ensure that desired results are achieved.


The organisation was borne out of necessity, to combat the menace of project abandonment, empowerment funds mismanagement; promote human development, profitable agricultural activities, seamless export and import services, and to improve the level of knowledge of ICT especially among the rural dwellers.


As sustainable contractors and empowerment experts, we undertake contracts and/or partnership deals with top government officials, agencies or private entities to TRAIN, MONITOR the progress of, and MENTOR beneficiaries of empowerment schemes so that the funding or material resources they eventually receive are put to economically profitable use.


We also accept contract deals to carry out infrastructural development projects. We consult on human empowerment, manufacturing, import and export, agricultural practice and ICT.
